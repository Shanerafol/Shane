Mabanag Spring Resort

Overview

Mabanag Spring Resort is a charming tourist attraction located in Barangay Tuboran, Mahayag, Zamboanga del Sur. It’s about six (6) kilometers from the town proper, conveniently accessible by all types of vehicles. The resort is adjacent to Eriberta Spring Resort, with a beautiful flow of clean, fresh, and translucent water. Surrounded by lush greenery, the resort offers a refreshing atmosphere and features affordable cottages with hygienic comfort rooms available for guests. Children can also enjoy swimming in the designated children's pools.

Features and Activities

The resort spans a total land area of 9.7 hectares, with some sections currently under construction. Visitors can appreciate the resort’s natural beauty, which includes landscaped ponds with colorful fish, singing birds, and lush greenery. A specially designed bridge over the swimming pool provides a relaxing spot for guests to unwind after swimming. The entrance fee is only 30 pesos for adults and 20 pesos for children. The resort also offers kayaking activities for 150 pesos per hour.

Accommodation Options

Mabanag Spring Resort features an array of colorful cottages surrounding the pools. Cottage rates are budget-friendly, with small cottages costing between 250 to 500 pesos, wide cottages priced at 1,000 pesos, and cottages with videoke available for 1,500 to 2,500 pesos. There are twenty (20) friendly staff members ready to welcome guests and ensure a pleasant stay.

Management

The resort is owned by Mr. Rogelio C. Mabanag, the former Punong Barangay of San Isidro, Mahayag. His dedicated staff members are known for their hospitality and friendliness, embodying the warm spirit of the Mahayagnon people.
