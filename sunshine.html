Sunshine Suites and Resort

Overview

Sunshine Suites and Resort, formerly known as Mandarin Suites and Resort, is the most popular accommodation establishment in the municipality of Mahayag. Its name is derived from the name of the ownerâ€™s daughter. It is nearby the Municipal Mall and very accessible to all types of vehicles. It has two (2) swimming pools, one suited for adults and the other for children. Visitors who want to enjoy swimming will pay a 50 pesos entrance fee, and there are also affordable cottages available.

Room Options

Sunshine Suites and Resort has eleven (11) fully air-conditioned rooms, including two (2) family-size rooms and nine (9) standard rooms. There are rooms ideal for two (2) persons with a rate of 500 pesos, for three (3) persons at 900 pesos, and for five (5) persons at 1,200 pesos. Each room includes a hygienic comfort room and television. The resort employs seven (7) staff members who warmly welcome and assist guests upon arrival and departure.

Management and Facilities

Sunshine Suites and Resort is owned and managed by Mr. John Lester Fanilag, the only son of the known businessman in this municipality, and it is officially accredited by the Department of Tourism. For those who love night swimming activities, this resort offers great satisfaction as guests can experience the coolness of the pools.
