Eriberta Spring Resort

Overview

Eriberta Spring Resort is gifted by an unceasing flow of water with a naturally cold, clean, pure, and transparent appearance. It comprises four (4) gigantic swimming pools intended for adults and children, as well as three (3) Jacuzzi pools for everyone who wants to relax. The natural beauty and symmetry of the resort, surrounded by greenery and large trees, offer a refreshing and breezy environment. Spacious cottages with affordable pricing are also available, with some cottages equipped with videoke for family gatherings and special occasions.

Location and Accessibility

This resort is located in Barangay Tuboran, approximately seven (7) kilometers from the town proper. It is accessible for all types of vehicles, ensuring a hassle-free visit. The resort occupies a total land area of eight (8) hectares, including a water reservoir. The entrance fee is affordable, costing 40 pesos for adults and 20 pesos for children. Cottages are priced between 150 pesos for the umbrella type and up to 2,000 pesos for functional halls with videoke.

Accommodation and Dining

Eriberta Spring Resort offers seven (7) standard rooms, including three (3) fully air-conditioned rooms and four (4) non-air-conditioned rooms, all at reasonable rates. Visitors can enjoy delicious meals at Mama Linda Kitchen, where scrumptious foods and refreshing drinks cater to guests' hunger and thirst.

Management and Staff

The resort is owned and managed by former Mayor of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur, Ex Mayor Flavio J. Saniel Jr. The staff are known for their warm hospitality and friendliness, ensuring a pleasant experience for all visitors. During holidays and vacation periods, many visitors flock to the resort to enjoy its captivating sights and the refreshing coldness of the water.
